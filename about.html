Aim of Our Team

At GenZ Finance, our aim is to empower and educate the younger generation about personal finance. We understand the unique financial challenges faced by young individuals and strive to provide accessible and tailored financial services to help you achieve your goals.

About the Founder

Silas, the founder of GenZ Finance, brings over three years of experience as a financial consultant and team leader in the insurance industry. With a proven track record, including three-time qualification for the prestigious MDRT (Million Dollar Round Table), Silas is dedicated to helping young individuals make informed financial decisions.

Having recognized the need for accessible financial services tailored to the younger generation, Silas decided to go independent and establish GenZ Finance - a comprehensive platform designed to assist and educate individuals like you in achieving your financial goals.
